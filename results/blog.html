HIT 3: Nani Unleashes the Beast in the Latest Thriller

Released Yesterday, This Action-Packed Installment Takes the Franchise to a New Level

The highly anticipated third installment in the successful HIT (Homicide Investigation Team) franchise, HIT: The Third Case, hit screens yesterday, May 1st, 2025. This time, the baton is passed to 'Natural Star' Nani, who steps into the shoes of the ruthless cop SP Arjun Sarkaar, promising a significant shift in his on-screen persona and the franchise's intensity.

A Darker, More Intense Chapter

Directed by Sailesh Kolanu, who helmed the previous two films, HIT 3 plunges audiences into a gripping investigation involving a series of brutal serial murders. Unlike the more procedural nature of the earlier films, this installment leans heavily into raw action and a darker narrative, with Nani's character operating with fierce determination and unconventional methods.

Nani's Electrifying Transformation

One of the biggest talking points surrounding HIT 3 is Nani's bold transformation into a full-fledged action hero. Known for his versatile roles, Nani reportedly delivers a powerful and commanding performance as Arjun Sarkaar, earning praise from many viewers for his intense portrayal. Reviews highlight his ability to carry the film with his presence, even amidst the film's high-octane action sequences.

Action, Violence, and Mixed Reactions

HIT 3 is being described as a pulsating survival thriller packed with brutal action. The film's violence level has resulted in an 'A' certification, indicating it's strictly for adult audiences. While action lovers and Nani fans seem to appreciate the intense sequences and his new avatar, some reviews note that the excessive bloodshed and a predictable plot in parts might be drawbacks. The film's second half is particularly noted for its action-heavy approach.

Building the HIT Universe

As part of the expanding HIT universe, the film features cameos from actors from previous installments, further connecting the cases. Crucially, the climax of HIT 3 reportedly sets up the stage for the next chapter, teasing the arrival of HIT 4 and leaving fans eager to see how the franchise will evolve.

The Verdict

HIT 3 is a film that seems to divide opinions. It's a must-watch for Nani fans eager to see him in a new, aggressive role and for audiences who enjoy intense, action-packed thrillers. However, those sensitive to graphic violence or looking for a complex investigative plot might find it less appealing. Despite mixed reviews on certain aspects, the film appears to have started strong, particularly in overseas markets, marking another significant film for Nani.
